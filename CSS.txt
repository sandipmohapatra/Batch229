CSS:- cascading style sheet
------------------------------------
It is used to style our html page.
We have 3 types of css.
1)inline css:- It will be applicable to a single line.
2)internal css:- It will be applicable to a single page.
3)external css:- It will be applicable to a single project.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The extention will be .html for inline and internal css.
The extention will be .css for external css.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
